Lam Ngo

Visiting Researcher | Research Engineer | CitAI Research Center | London, UK

I am currently a Visiting Researcher at CitAI Research Center (London). I am collaborating with PhD student Vasilis Siomos and Dr. Giacomo Tarroni on a project investigating image clustering methods to create non-IID data for federated learning in computer vision tasks, both for natural images and medical images.

I held a BSc in Math and an MSc in Computer Science. My thesis work, supervised by Dr. Chris Child, focused on building environment models for reinforcement learning tasks using dynamic programming and features extractions from neural networks. The work was published in the proceedings of ISNN 2025 (International Symposium on Neural Networks)

My research interests include: 2D/3D Computer Vision, Generative AI, AI for Medical Application, and Reinforcement Learning.
